About Me

Hi, I'm Mackenzie, a passionate full-stack developer with a love for creating beautiful, functional, and user-friendly applications.

I specialize in modern web technologies including React, Next.js, Astro, Node.js, and TypeScript. My goal is to write clean, maintainable code that not only works well but is also enjoyable to work with.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my knowledge through blog posts and tutorials.
